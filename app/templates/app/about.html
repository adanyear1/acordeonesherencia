About Us!

The Acordeones Herencia Story

Acordeones Herencia is a subsidiary of Herencia Industries that provides beautiful accordions to both student and professional musicians. Due to Enrique's Love of the accordion and Manuel's background in manufacturing and design the duo decided to design a piano accordion that will satisfy the needs of student, and professional musicians. In the fall of 2022 the first accordion from Acordeones Herencia became available in the market, being loved by aspiring, student and professional accordionists due to their loud beautiful notes and modern design.

Our mission is to create high quality accordions for musicians of all levels. We strive to provide an accordion that our customers can use for years to come and inherit to their children and grandchildren.

Our vision is to be a global provider of accordions to musicians worldwide who are looking to play the music that was inherited by them by their culture.
